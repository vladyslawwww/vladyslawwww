### ⚙️ what i actually do (and how i do it)

i bridge the gap between heavy enterprise operations and modern ai capabilities.

* **the problem:** large companies rely on rigid legacy template-based ocr tools or slow workflows for processing thousands of complex documents, invoices, and tables. they break on edge-cases.
* **my solution:** i build custom **ocr & vlm (vision-language model) engines** from scratch.
* **how it works under the hood:** i write asynchronous **python** pipelines deployed on serverless **azure durable functions**. i use **azure ai foundry** for data extraction, but i wrap the ai outputs in strict **pydantic** schemas and heuristic fallbacks. this guarantees 100% predictable, structured json before the data ever hits the company's erp system.
